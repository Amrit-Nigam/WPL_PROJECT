OUR STORY

Orion Racing India was founded in 2006 by a group of passionate engineering students from KJ Somaiya College of Engineering with a vision to design and build formula-style race cars and compete in international Formula Student competitions.

Over the years, we have grown into a team of 60+ dedicated members working across various departments including Design, Manufacturing, Electronics, Marketing, and Management.

Our team has participated in numerous Formula Student competitions across the globe, including Formula Student UK, Formula Student Germany, and Formula Student India, consistently improving our performance and gaining valuable experience.

OUR MISSION

To provide students with a platform to apply theoretical knowledge to practical engineering challenges, fostering innovation and teamwork while building world-class formula-style race cars.

OUR VISION

To be recognized as one of the top Formula Student teams globally, known for innovation, engineering excellence, and fostering the next generation of automotive engineers.
