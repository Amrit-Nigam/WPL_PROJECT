ABOUT ORION RACING

Orion Racing India is the Formula Student team of KJ Somaiya College of Engineering, dedicated to designing, building, and racing formula-style vehicles in international competitions.

Our team comprises passionate engineering students who apply classroom knowledge to real-world challenges, developing innovative solutions and pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering.
